Steps to learning

There are 6 strings on most guitars, classic tuning from top to bottom are:

E A D G B E

This is traditional tuning and for the most part this is the tuning your guitar will be in. Some songs and generes (heavy metal) use drop D tuning where the first string is tuned down to D, or other tuning variations. There are some guitars which have more strings 7 or 8 or double strings where each string has a smaller secound string adjecent to it making a total of twelve string on the guitar. Playing the strings will at first hurt your fingers but slowly your hand will get use to it!
