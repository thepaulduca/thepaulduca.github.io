Steps to Learning Guitar

  1. Buy guitar
  2. Learn the parts of the guitar
    • Body
    • Neck
    • Frets
    • Tuning nobs
    • Pickkups (electric only)
    • Volume/Tone nobs (electric only)
  3. Try strumming without putting fingers on the frets
  4. Learn to play smoke on the water
